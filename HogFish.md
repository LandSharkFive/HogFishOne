# The Hog Fish

Deep among the seafan glades,
Where reef and current slip,
Glides a pale and shifting phantom
With a long and tapered lip.

A hogfish on the hunt today,
In a coat of pearly light;
But brush a patch of rusty sponge—
He’ll vanish from your sight.

The bags are sent, the hunt is done,
We drift where shadows die,
To hang within the silvered glass,
Beneath the ocean skies.
